# Examples: Islamic Manuscript Decoration Types

Visual reference images organized by decoration category, sourced from open-access museum collections. Each image is named with the pattern `{source}_{accession}_{description}.jpg`.

---

## Page-level illumination

### Serlevha / ʿunwān (illuminated headpiece)

- `met_451285_shah_jahan_unwan.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album ʿunwān (Met 55.121.10.38). Mughal, ca. 1630–40. Lavish floral arabesques framing calligraphy columns.

### Zahriye (frontispiece)

- `met_452669_bustan_frontispiece.jpg` — Illuminated frontispiece of a Bustan of Saʿdi (Met). Central medallion with geometric and vegetal designs.
- `met_451015_baghdad_quran_opening.jpg` — Opening folio of the 26th volume of the "Anonymous Baghdad Qur'an" (Met). Ilkhanid-era illumination.
- `loc_2019714481_illuminated_frontispiece.jpg` — Illuminated frontispiece (Library of Congress). Central medallion surrounded by geometric and vegetal patterns.

### Cedvel / jadwal (ruling and frame lines)

- `met_454000_quran_section_ruling.jpg` — Section from a Qur'an (Met). Multi-layered ruling and frame-line systems around text block.

## Corner and margin elements

### Koltuk (corner pieces / spandrel illumination)

- `met_451992_ottoman_quran_corners.jpg` — Ottoman Qur'an, reign of Mehmed II (Met 2001.408). Corner pieces with medallions.

### Gül (rosette verse markers)

- `met_447781_quran_illuminated_rosettes.jpg` — Section of a Qur'an (Met 24.146.1). Illuminated rosette verse markers and marginal decoration throughout 274 folios.

## Border systems

### İç pervaz / ara suyu (inner border band)

- `met_451265_shah_jahan_inner_border.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album fol. 10.2 (Met 55.121.10.2). Narrow inner border of gold palmette scrolls on blue ground.
- `met_454624_shah_jahan_inner_border_fol10-17.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album fol. 10.17 (Met 55.121.10.17). Inner border of gold palmette scrolls on blue ground.

### Dış pervaz / kenar suyu (outer border band)

- `met_451294_shah_jahan_outer_border.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album folio (Met 55.121.10.5). Wide outer border with dense gold flowers — poppies, tulips, iris.

## Paper decoration

### Zerefşân / serpme (gold-sprinkled paper)

- `met_452176_shahnameh_tahmasp_folio.jpg` — Shahnameh of Shah Tahmasp, folio 595v (Met 1970.301.67). Gold-sprinkled margins visible around painting.
- `met_452176_shahnameh_tahmasp_verso.jpg` — Verso of same folio showing gold-sprinkled paper in text margins.

### Ebrî / ebrulu (marbled paper)

- `met_453166_album_shaikh_hamdullah_ebru.jpg` — Album of Calligraphies by Shaikh Hamdullah (Met 1982.120.3), ca. 1500. Multiple ebru patterns: combed peacocks, getgels, stone/speckled.

### Mülevven (colored paper)

- `met_454343_cut_paper_blue_ground.jpg` — Album Page with Cut-Paper Decoration (Met 2002.458). Mughal, Shah Jahan period. Tan cut-paper on blue paper background.

### Silhouetted paper

- WhatsApp images (4 files) — Physical examples of silhouetted paper with floral/animal motifs.

## Margin painting

### Halkâr / halkârî (shaded gold floral painting in margins)

- `met_451265_shah_jahan_halkar_fol10-2.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album fol. 10.2 (Met 55.121.10.2). Border by illuminator Daulat: flower heads brushed with gold, dark outlines on stems and leaves. Also serves as iç pervaz example.

### Teşʿîr / tashʿīr (fine gold line drawings in margins)

- `met_451286_shah_jahan_shamsa_tashir.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album shamsa folio (Met 55.121.10.39). Marginal decorations with mythical animals, simurgh, and arabesques in gold leaf.

### Illuminated margins (tezhipli kenar)

- `met_447781_quran_illuminated_margins.jpg` — Section of a Qur'an (Met 24.146.1). Polychrome border systems with vegetal scrolls on deep blue grounds.

## Binding decoration

### Şemse / shamsa (central medallion)

- `met_455249_mantiq_al_tayr_binding.jpg` — Binding for the Mantiq al-Tayr (Met 63.210). Safavid, ca. 1600. Central medallion, quadrants, and border panels.
- `met_451368_quran_binding_turquoise.jpg` — Qur'an Bookbinding Inset with Turquoise (Met 56.48). Safavid, 16th c. Almond-shaped central medallion.

### Köşebend (corner pieces on bindings)

- `cleveland_1944_495_safavid_quran_binding.jpg` — Bookbinding for a Qur'an (Cleveland 1944.495). Safavid, ca. 1525–50. Central medallion with prominent corner pieces.

### Ruganî / lake (lacquer-painted bindings)

- `met_455076_qajar_lacquer_binding.jpg` — Qajar lacquer bookbinding by Aqa Buzurg Shirazi (Met 91.1.535). Mid-19th c. Central medallion over scrolling vegetation.

### Soğuk şemse (blind-stamped medallion)

- `met_448906_mamluk_binding.jpg` — Mamluk bookbinding (Met 33.14). 14th c. Blind-tooled interlacing geometric pattern from a central star medallion.

## Motif families

### Rūmī (split-leaf palmettes)

- `met_449533_tughra_suleiman.jpg` — Tughra of Sultan Süleiman the Magnificent (Met 38.149.1), ca. 1555–60. Split-leaf rumi scrolls fill the tughra interior alongside saz leaves and cloud bands.

### Bulut / cloud band

- `met_449533_tughra_suleiman_cloud_bands.jpg` — Same tughra (Met 38.149.1). Cloud-scroll bands visible between the extending strokes.

### Saz style (serrated leaves with composite flowers)

- `met_451405_dragon_amid_foliage_shahkulu.jpg` — Dragon amid Foliage by Shah Quli/Şahkulu (Met 57.51.26), ca. 1540–50. Definitive saz-style composition with serrated leaves and dragon.

### Girih (geometric interlace patterns)

- `met_454664_quran_geometric_frontispiece.jpg` — Section from a Qur'an Manuscript (Met). Geometric frontispiece with interlocking star-and-polygon patterns.

## Special formats

### Murakkaʿ (calligraphy/painting album)

- `met_451287_shah_jahan_album_page.jpg` — Shah Jahan Album page (Met 55.121.10.x). Calligraphy with illuminated animals and plants in a field of flowers.
- `walters_w672_ottoman_calligraphy_album.jpg` — Album of Ottoman Calligraphy (Walters W.672). Ottoman accordion-format muraqqa.

### Mushaf (decorated Qur'an)

- `met_454662_blue_quran.jpg` — Folio from the Blue Qur'an (Met 2004.88). Late 9th–mid 10th c. Gold Kufic on indigo parchment.
